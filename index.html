üìò About AIXR-Coach

Traditional industrial training often falls short: it's generic, slow to adapt, and struggles to replicate real-world complexities. This leads to inefficiencies, increased risks, and a disconnect between theoretical knowledge and practical application. Current XR solutions frequently suffer from immersion-breaking latency and lack true personalization.

AIXR-Coach revolutionizes this paradigm. We design a cross-device XR training experience that uses AI to personalise human learning and includes multisensory feedback in real time, while predicting and optimising latency to keep immersion consistent. Our system provides an adaptive training experience for critical industrial production tasks, from large-scale engine assembly to precision hearing aid manufacturing.

Our core objective is to improve **safety, quality, and efficiency** in industrial environments by offering a training platform that understands and adapts to each individual learner.

‚ú® Core Features

  • Adaptive Learning Journeys:
    • AI personalizes the experience based on individual worker profiles, past performance, and task history.
    • The first training run is uniquely tailored, and the seventh run adapts based on prior sessions, ensuring continuous improvement.
    • Incorporates worker language preferences with live translation capabilities.
    • Awareness mechanisms identify critical process points, judged by past successes and incidents.
  • Real-time Multisensory Feedback:
    • Augmented information is displayed directly in the worker's view (XR overlay).
    • Haptic feedback guides precise movements and alerts.
    • Spatial audio provides clear instructions, warnings, and environmental cues.
    • Safety-Critical Warnings: Immediate visual, haptic, and audio alerts for safety rule infringements or proximity to hazards.
  • Seamless Cross-Device Immersion:
    • Supports diverse XR hardware (Meta Quest 3, Apple Vision Pro, AR glasses) with local on-device computation for low communication overhead.
    • Enables shared virtual training sessions where multiple workers or a worker and supervisor can experience the same environment in real-time.
    • Remote Supervision: Supervisors can remotely "jump into" a worker's point-of-view, offering instant guidance or intervention, especially during safety-critical events. Workers can also request supervision.
  • Predictive Latency Optimization:
    • Our AI predicts and actively optimizes latency across all devices to maintain a consistent and uninterrupted immersive experience.
    • Leverages local computation on sophisticated AR devices to minimize communication delays.
  • Scalable & Modular System:
    • Companies can easily integrate their own specific cases, scenarios, and training modules.

ü§ù Ethics & Inclusivity

We are committed to developing AIXR-Coach responsibly, with a strong focus on ethical considerations and inclusivity:

  • Data Privacy: Implementing robust pseudonymization and anonymization pipelines. If recordings are kept, faces and sensitive information are blurred.
  • Aggregated Metrics: Focusing on aggregated results and system improvements, avoiding personal worker metric "nightmares."
  • Consent & Control: Supervisors can *request* access to a worker's POV, ensuring transparency and worker consent.
  • Inclusivity by Design: Considering diverse user needs, including gender-neutral design, customizable color schemes for visual impairments, adjustable interaction weights, and support for various physical capabilities.
